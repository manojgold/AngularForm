How HoSh Fundraising works


1 Choose

My Share to contribute for the need

  • View needs for donation
  • Select a need for donation
  • Enter a gift amount
  • Register or Login and make a donation
2 Referral

Share it with your friends and family

  • View details of your donation on your donor information page
  • Volunteer by asking friends to donate to the cause that you have selected
3 Monitor

Track your invitees

  • Once the need amount is fully met, project implementation will begin and timely updates can be viewed on your donor information page
4 Notification

Get Emails on the Need status and report

  • See the change you brought about. View pictures of the completed project and the beneficiaries on your donor information page
  • Tell your friends about HoSh
