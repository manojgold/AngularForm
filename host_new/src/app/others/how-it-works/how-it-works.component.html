How It Works

How HoSh crowdfunding works


1 Choose a Need
  • Select the cause/gifts
  • Register/Login
2 Make Your Donation
  • Enter the amount you would like to contribute. Other donors like you will add to your contribution until the need amount is met.
3 Track Progress
  • Get email notifications, once the fund has been raised for the specific need
  • Receive timely updates about the need execution and completion
4 Receive a Report
  • Receive a detailed report on how your donation was used.
