How Fundraising Works

1 Register/Login

You can register with your Email ID.

2 Select a cause

Choose a cause that matters to you. It could be across a particular Need Type (Individual Needs, Large Needs, Recurring Gifts, etc) or across categories like Health, Water & Sanitation, Economic Development, Education or Gifts for Children with Disability.

3 Invite your friends

  • Choose a need and the amount to be raised.
  • Add a description on why you chose that need.
  • Share with your friends through social network/email.
  • You can send reminders as often as you want.

4 Track Need Progress

  • Receive status e-mails.
  • Track the progress.
  • Get email notifications, once the fund has been raised for the specific need.

5 Stay updated!

  • Receive timely updates about the need execution and completion.
  • Receive a detailed report that will feature the journey map of the need chosen, the amount paid, the action implemented, pictures/videos of beneficiary and beneficiary testimonials.
